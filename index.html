Some cool facts about camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Some cool facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  • Chameleons live in warm varied habitats from rainforests through to deserts.

Some cool facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors

Some cool facts about Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they only eat bamboo and fruit
